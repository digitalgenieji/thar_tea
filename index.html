Our Heritage

About Royal Thar Tea

Royal Thar Tea brings the essence of Rajasthan's golden sands to your cup. Using the finest Assam tea leaves, we create a blend that honors our cultural heritage while offering a bold and smooth flavor for modern tastes.

Our Story

Born from a passion for authentic Indian tea, Royal Thar Tea started as a small family endeavor and has grown into a symbol of quality and tradition. Every cup you enjoy is part of a journey that began in the heart of the Thar Desert.
